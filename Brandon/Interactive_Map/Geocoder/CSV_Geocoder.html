CSV Address Geocoder

Upload a CSV with addresses and convert to GeoJSON coordinates

Upload a CSV file with your addresses. The tool will auto-detect columns for name, address, city, state, and zip. You can adjust the mapping if needed.

Processing speed: To avoid rate limiting, the tool uses a 5-second delay between requests plus a 10-second pause every 30 records. For 250+ records, expect 25-30 minutes total processing time.
